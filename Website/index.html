CSCI 452: Compiler & Interpreter Construction

LL Parser

LL Parser is a top-down parser (also predictive parsing). Recursive descent parsing is a top-down parser.

To do top down parsing, you must transform a grammer in two ways:

  1. eliminate left recursion
  2. perform left factoring (remove non deterministic)

A simple grammar

  1. Expression → Term
  2. Term → Term - Term | Term + Term | Factor
  3. Factor → Factor * Factor | Factor / Factor | ( Expression ) | var | int | real
